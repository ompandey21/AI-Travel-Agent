## 🚀 What is Iternation?
**Iternation** is an AI-driven end-to-end travel management platform that helps groups plan, manage, and execute trips without chaos.  
From smart itineraries to expense splitting and hidden-gem discovery — everything lives in one place.

---

## 🧠 Why Iternation?
- ❌ Fragmented planning across apps  
- ❌ Confusing group expenses  
- ❌ Safety concerns & unknown areas  
- ❌ Hidden places never discovered  

➡️ **Iternation fixes it all.**
